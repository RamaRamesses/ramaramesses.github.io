Before starting the game, please read the guide below first.

Keep striking/dodging until the bar highlight changes.

Press 'E' to start the game

Controls

Press "A" to DODGE

Press "D" to STRIKE

How to play

You are playing as the red robot

Hit STRIKE when above bar says PUNCH!

DODGE when the bar says NO!

You will get hit if you do not follow the order!

Important Hint, read first!

You are required to STRIKE/DODGE multiple times until the above bar highlight changed, otherwise you'll still get hit, but make sure you do it once in a while

Do not spam the controls, as it will make you more vulnerable.

Opponent mechanism will automatically lock your control and parry as you abuse the attack/dodge key

Dicipline is required
